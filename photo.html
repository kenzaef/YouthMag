Take pictures of people in your life

To be honest, I can’t give a correct reason why you should take pictures of others. A lot of times, I just wish people would take pictures of me. Not for the social media, also not for framing them. But for the memories.

For that one outfit I spent my whole day planning and never actually knew what it looked like on me. For that realy funny moment when someone told a joke and everyone started howling. Or maybe for the day I found my lost cat and was crying with her in my arms.

I don't want the pictures because I’m afraid I won't remember these events, I want them so I can see the way the world and I looked like in one special moment when the shutter went off.

The cool thing with photography is you can catch a complex moment full of emotion and freze it in time, in a perfect and still representation of it. When everyting seemed to stop and pose for the camera.

december 2019

As a photographer, I strive to tell a story. Sometimes it happens without actually trying. All it took to transform a basic picture of my boyfriend getting a haircut into a powerful, personal portrait was one look at the perfect timing.

The chemistry between the lens and the subject is sometimes all it takes for a picture to be meaningful. When the subject I am photographing has a good energy that mixes well with mine and the environment, a good shot is born. If you’re having a bad day taking photos and can’t really get the result you want, ask yourself if you or your subject are actually putting out the right energy. Sometimes it’s simply the wrong time.

august 2020

It’s not always so deep. Actually most of the times I just take pictures because I enjoy it. I enjoy seeing my friends living their best lives and being able to capture that feeling forever. I enjoy creating memories and giving anyone the oppoertunity to relive them.

I think you should take pictures of your friends anytime you get the chance. You never know when you might shoot something magic. The power is all in your hands, and years from now you’ll have a still from an ordinary night that just reminds you of the good and simple times you spent in your youth, surrounded by people you love.
