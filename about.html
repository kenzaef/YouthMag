Who am I?

My name is Kenza and I am a Multimedia Design stuent at Business Academy Aarhus. I have a passion for art, specifically photography, cinema and graphic design. I am a self taught video producer, learning video editing around 5 years ago through YouTube tutorials.

I am currently learning web development and creating this online magazine has been an interesting challenge for me. Being more comfortable with content creation, rather than web design, I found it much easier to write articles and provide visual material, than to program. As I started coding, however, I became more and more comfortable and intrigued by coding, so I can confidently say creating this magazine has opened the door to a new possible passion.

About the magazine

YouthMag was created for the artistic and conscious youth of today. It is made for and by students or young people in general, wanting a reliable and relatable information outlet. It is a rich and varied magazine, compiling multiple topics of interest for our target audience.

YouthMag tries to create a safe space for all youth to authentically express themselves and adapt to life as an adult. Mental health, student hacks and cooking are a few of the topics we are covering, in hopes of building a valuable database for people in need.
